Who We Are

Youth MOVE RI is a non-profit organization, and a chapter of the larger organization Youth M.O.V.E. National. The MOVE in Youth MOVE stands for Motivating Others through Voices of Experience and that's exactly what we try to do. At Youth MOVE RI we believe in using a peer model to provide support to anyone who has, or who might be, facing challenges with mental health, substance abuse, oppression, homelessness, and all other forms of adversity. Youth MOVE RI is run by youth and young adults who have lived experience and are dedicated to bringing youth voice and youth perspective to all of the conversations that concern us, our futures, and our peers.
